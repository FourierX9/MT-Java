Welcome to Calm Thinking

In this study, we are going to focus on how you think about situations that may make you anxious.

You will complete an online program that will describe different situations and encourage you to think about how those situations will turn out. This program is new but is based on similar programs that have shown positive effects in changing anxious thinking.

Because the program is still being tested, we have people trying different versions of it to see which version works best — we think some of these programs will lead to a healthier evaluation of the future.

Let's get started!
