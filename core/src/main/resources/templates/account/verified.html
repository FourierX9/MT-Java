Tango Card: Select Country





The Calm Thinking Study uses Tango Card, an awards delivery program, for distribution of e-gift cards to participants. With a Tango e-gift card, you can choose to use your awards among a range of retailers and services (such as Amazon, Apple App Store & iTunes, Nike, Uber, and more) or donate the value to a range of non-profits (including the American Cancer Society, Clean Water Fund, and Habitat for Humanity).

Tango Card offers 14 country-facing options, which you can select from below. Values are awarded in USD – options in other currencies will be converted from USD based on current exchange rates. Please view this link for more information on different Reward Link options offered for the United States, and this link for Reward Link options offered for other countries. Note that by navigating to "Verify My Phone" in the Account menu, you will be able to change your chosen country-facing option for future awards.
