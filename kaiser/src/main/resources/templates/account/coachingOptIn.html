Thank you for participating in the MindTrails program! You have the option to talk with a personal coach via text or phone call to help you with the program. Coaching is completely optional, and you can decide to continue participating in the MindTrails program even if you don’t want to talk with a coach.

If you would like to know more about the MindTrails personal coaches, you can view short bios in the 'MindTrails Coaches' section on the Meet the Team page (this link will open in a new web browser tab).


How Does Coaching Work?

  • Coaches are trained members of the MindTrails team, but are not trained therapists.
  • Coaches offer weekly check-ins via text message or phone call (whichever you prefer).
  • Coaches can explain more about how MindTrails works, address any concerns you have with any of the exercises, help troubleshoot technical issues, and provide reminders for sessions.
  • Coaching check-ins last about 10 minutes, and are scheduled for a time that fits with your schedule.
  • Coaching is completely voluntary and you may decide at any time that you no longer wish to work with a coach.

If you are interested in having a coach, one of our team members will reach out to you via email to schedule your first check-in after you finish your first MindTrails training.
