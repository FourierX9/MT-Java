The purpose of this part of the study is to investigate the effectiveness of a new, experimental program to encourage healthy thinking about the future. It includes 6 15-minute sessions, completed over the course of 1 week. You will be asked to complete a task in which you read brief stories, answer questions, and complete word fragments by filling in one or more missing letters in a word. Some of the stories will include an image in order to help you to imagine being in the described situation.

You can do the sessions anywhere, so long as you have access to a quiet place and a computer, phone or tablet with Internet.
